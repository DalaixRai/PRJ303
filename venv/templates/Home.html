    Materials Used

    DC, Motor

    A DC motor converts electrical energy into mechanical motion through magnetic interactions, rotating when DC voltage is applied. In water icing control systems, DC motors drive components to prevent freezing, providing reliable mechanical control.

    LED

    An LED is utilized to indicate power status or operation, offering a visual cue of system activity or readiness.

    Solar Panel

    A small solar panel harnesses sunlight to generate electrical power, suitable for charging batteries or powering small devices.

    ESP-32

    The ESP32 microcontroller is employed to collect sensor data and transmit it to a dashboard or remote system for monitoring and analysis.

    DHT Sensor

    water_temperature and humidity sensors like the DHT11 and DHT22 are used in water sources to simultaneously measure both water_temperature and humidity levels, providing comprehensive environmental data for analysis and control purposes.

    Power

    The Kamisafe 8000mAh Solar Power Kit is employed to supply power to the ESP32 microcontroller, sensors, and DC motors, offering a sustainable and portable energy solution for remote monitoring and control applications.

    Current

    Water current sensors measure water flow rates or speeds between different points in a system, such as from the water source to Tank 1 and from Tank 1 to Tank 2, providing essential data for monitoring and managing water distribution and usage.

    Ultrasonic

    Utilized in water level monitoring systems to measure distances accurately using sound waves, enabling precise monitoring and control of water levels in various applications..

    Relay

    A relay is used to control the voltage supply and direction of DC motors by switching electrical circuits on and off based on control signals, enabling precise speed control and direction reversal in motor applications.
